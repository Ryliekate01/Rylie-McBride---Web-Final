About Me

ryliemcbride@live.com | 346 492 2244

Home Life

I grew up in Spring, Texas, a suburb north of Houston. My father is a lawyer, and my mom managed a bank branch after receiving her MBA, but she became a stay-at-home mom after she had children. I have two sisters and two brothers, and we all love hanging out with each other. I had the opportunity to participate in many different activities while growing up, and I appreciate all the beautiful memories I have.

Education

After graduating high school, I came to Brigham Young University. I enjoyed BYU and loved all the memories I made. In my first year of college, I studied Family Life intending to become a Family Marriage Therapist. As a freshman, I had the opportunity to be a research assistant to Dr. Quintin Hunt, a professor in the marriage family therapy program. During that time, I realized that therapy might not be for me. Soon after my second semester ended, I served a mission for the Church of Jesus Christ. I served for 18 months in Salt Lake City, Spanish-speaking. After, I returned to BYU and am currently pursuing Experience Design and Management. I hope to go into event planning and management.

Hobbies

I love to run and swim. I grew up competing in both sports, and while I am not as good as I used to be, I still enjoy doing both. I also love to watercolor, scrapbook, write, and read. I am currently working on publishing my first book. My true love, though, is to plan events for my friends. This semester alone, we had a casino night, PowerPoint night, murder mystery night, St. Patrick's Day fire, and much more. These events allow me to connect with many people and make some memories that we know will last.
